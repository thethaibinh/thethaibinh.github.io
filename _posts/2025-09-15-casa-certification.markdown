Obtained the **Remote Pilot Licence (RePL)** and **Aeronautical Radio Operator Certificate (AROC)** issued by the **Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA)**, Australia.

These certifications authorise the operation of remotely piloted aircraft systems (RPAS) in Australian airspace, covering regulatory compliance, operational risk management, and aeronautical radio communications, essential credentials for conducting outdoor flight testing and field deployment of autonomous aerial platforms.
