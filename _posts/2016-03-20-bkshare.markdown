BK-Share is a club I founded back in 2016 at Hanoi University of Science and Technology, born from a simple idea: creating a community where engineering students could sharpen their programming skills together.

There was one class "Computational Programming and Simulation with MATLAB/Simulink" and two "Microcontroller Programming and Fundamental Circuit Design" organized, serving more than 60 undergraduate students across various fields in the campus.
